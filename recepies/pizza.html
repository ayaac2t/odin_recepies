Pizza

Description

The pizza is a beloved Italian dish it's very delicious and easy to make. so here's your sign to make your pizza at home and enjoy it.

Ingedients

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • olive oil
  • salt

The pizza sauce

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano/Italian seasoning
  • Garlic salt
  • Granulated sugar

The steps

  1. mix it all up in a bowl.
  2. knead by hand for 3 minutes,or just blitz it all together in a food processor for 40 seconds.

the sauce

  1. mix the tomato paste and sauce together in a medium size bowl until well combined
  2. add the rest of the ingredients
  3. mix until the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the sauce
  4. use it on your pizza it's ready to go!
